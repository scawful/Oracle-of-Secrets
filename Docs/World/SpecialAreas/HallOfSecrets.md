# Hall of Secrets

**Type:** Special Area
**Entrance ID(s):** 0x02, 0x12
**OW Screen:** 0x0E (main), 0x02 (pyramid route)
**Purpose:** Farore's Sanctuary

---

## Overview

The Hall of Secrets serves as the central narrative hub for Oracle of Secrets. This is where the Oracle Farore resides and provides guidance throughout the player's journey. The Hall functions similarly to the Chamber of Sages in other Zelda titles.

---

## Features

### Oracle Farore

- Provides story guidance
- Unlocks abilities at key milestones
- Reveals secrets about the three Oracles (Din, Nayru, Farore)

### Pyramid Route (0x12)

A secret back entrance accessible from OW 0x02. May be used for:
- Speedrun routes
- Secret sequence breaks
- Post-game content

---

## Story Significance

The Hall of Secrets is visited at these story points:

1. **After Intro:** Farore explains the quest
2. **After First Pendant:** Ability unlock
3. **After All Pendants:** Master Sword guidance
4. **After All Dungeons:** Final dungeon access
5. **Post-Game:** Secret endings
